From the Director's Desk

Mr. Sankineni Sridhar Rao is a person with integrity, humility and dedication a name which spells supreme quality in the fields of academics and administration. He has been rendering his untiring services as the principal of Pragathi Degree College (Co-Ed). He inspires and supported generations of students and stood as a role model for his intellectual curiosity and passion for knowledge. He left a lasting impression on each and every student studied in Pragathi Degree college He has always played a pivotal role in the development of Pragathi Degree college (Co-Ed). He is idolized by many, and is a intellectual par excellence who strongly advocated for education which involved learning, critical thinking, analysis, research and reasoning. He is a co-founder and an active member of the director board of Pragathi Educational institutions. He is a renowned academician with great human virtues
