Administration for Degree

Major D. Ratnaji

A Guiding Force

At Pragathi Degree College, one name that commands deep respect among students and faculty alike is Major D. Ratnaji Sir. A man of discipline, vision, and dedication, he plays a dual and crucial role in shaping the future of the institution. As the officer who looks after college admissions, he ensures that the process is fair, transparent, and student-friendly. His approachable nature makes it easy for students and parents to seek guidance, and his firm yet kind manner helps many young learners step confidently into the next stage of their education. Beyond academics, Major Ratnaji is the heart and spirit of the National Cadet Corps (NCC) at Pragathi. Under his leadership, cadets are trained not just in drills and parades but in values of patriotism, courage, and leadership. He motivates students to participate in camps, secure NCC certificates, and explore career opportunities in defense and public service. His own military background brings authenticity and inspiration to the training he provides. For the students of Pragathi Degree College, Major D. Ratnaji is more than an administrator; he is a mentor, a role model, and a living example of discipline blended with compassion. His contribution continues to strengthen the college’s reputation for excellence in both academics and character building.

One must act, and act rightly. This has been the guiding principle with which Pragathi Group of Institutions was established. Since then, in just over a decade, our institution has emerged as the preferred choice of students for Higher Education at the Undergraduate and Postgraduate levels. This is because we emphasize sound fundamentals and thorough training that is practical in content and contemporary in outlook.
