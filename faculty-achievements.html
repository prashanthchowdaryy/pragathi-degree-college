Faculty Achievements

Recognizing the outstanding contributions of our esteemed faculty.

swatch Bharat Abhiyan NCC

The Bharat Abhiyan conducted by the National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a meaningful campaign that unites youth in service and national duty. Through this Abhiyan, NCC cadets organise cleanliness drives, awareness marches, and public pledge ceremonies in their towns and colleges. They create posters, hold debates, and disseminate messages via social media about important causes such as clean environment, drug-free life, or self-reliance. The initiative strengthens civic sense among young people, inspiring them to serve the community and uphold discipline. Under the leadership of NCC officers, cadets become role models in their neighborhoods. Ultimately, Bharat Abhiyan is about turning patriotism into action, about realizing that small efforts by many can lead to big change for the country.

NCC Cadets Leading to Police Careers

Many students from Pragathi Degree College have used their NCC training as a stepping stone to join the police force. Through drills, camps, and leadership programs, cadets gain discipline, fitness, and confidence, making them well-prepared for police exams and service. NCC instills courage, teamwork, and a sense of duty, helping students transform into responsible citizens ready to serve the nation.
